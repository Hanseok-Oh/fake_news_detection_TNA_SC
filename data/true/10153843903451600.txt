 Ivanka Trump will huddle Tuesday afternoon with just over a dozen female Republican Senators and House members on Capitol Hill, multiple sources told POLITICO. The GOP presidential nominee’s daughter will talk more about her father’s new childcare policy platform, which she helped create. She'll also hear from women in Congress about their own ideas and proposals related to family issues, women in the workplace, job flexibility and equal pay, one source added.
